### 14 peptide profiles (substance captured)

1. **BPC-157** — recovery/gut; angiogenesis + GH-receptor upregulation in
   damaged tissue; chronic injury, gut (leaky gut/IBS/SIBO), post-surgical.
2. **TB-500** (thymosin β4) — healing/mobility; actin upregulation → cell
   migration to injury sites; inflammation, range of motion, overuse.
3. **CJC-1295** — fat loss + muscle; GHRH analogue → sustained GH pulses;
   recomposition, anti-aging.
4. **Ipamorelin** — sleep + recovery; selective ghrelin-mimetic GH release,
   no cortisol/prolactin effect.
5. **Semaglutide** — appetite control; GLP-1 mimetic; slowed gastric
   emptying, insulin response, central appetite; ~10–15% body-weight loss
   over 12–24 wk in trials.
6. **Tirzepatide** — metabolic health; dual GIP/GLP-1 agonist; up to ~22%
   body-weight reduction in trials.
7. **AOD-9604** — targeted fat loss; modified HGH fragment → lipolysis
   without insulin effect or tissue growth.
8. **MK-677 (Ibutamoren)** — oral GH secretagogue (ghrelin mimetic),
   sustained GH pulse amplitude; no injection.
9. **GHK-Cu** — skin/hair; 40+ yr research, ~4,000 genes in tissue
   remodeling; collagen/elastin synthesis, follicle proliferation.
10. **PT-141 (Bremelanotide)** — vitality/libido; CNS melanocortin-receptor
    activation in hypothalamus (not vascular).
11. **DSIP** — deep sleep; lowers cortisol/ACTH, promotes delta-wave
    activity; antioxidant/neuroendocrine modulation.
12. **Kisspeptin** — hormone balance; master reproductive regulator,
    GnRH→LH/FSH/sex-hormone cascade.
13. **Epitalon** — longevity; telomerase activation framing; long-term
    cyclic use in studies.
14. **IGF-1 LR3** — muscle/cellular repair; IGF-1 receptor → hyperplasia
    (new muscle cells) vs hypertrophy.
